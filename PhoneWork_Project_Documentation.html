Job Completion OTP System

üîê Secure Two-Way Verification

When a job is completed, the worker requests completion through the app. The system generates a unique OTP which is shared with the Work Giver. The worker enters this OTP to verify job completion, ensuring both parties confirm the work is done. This prevents disputes and fraud.
